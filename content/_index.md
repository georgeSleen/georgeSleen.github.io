## About Me

I am a highly motivated engineering physics student with a passion for electronics! Over the course of my degree at UBC I will specialize in electrical engineering and applied solid state physics.

Currently I am on a co-op work term with <a href="https://www.bluerobotics.com">Blue Robotics</a> for electronic design. I feel extremely lucky to work with the research and development team in Victoria on the future of marine robotics!

I am the current captain of SUBC, UBC's human powered submarine design team, where I help over 50 other students work towards creating a fully functioning submarine. This summer we will compete in the 18th International Submarine Races in Maryland, USA. Check out <a href="https://www.subc.ca">subc.ca</a> for more infomation about SUBC and ISR18.

Outside of school, I enjoy building small projects when I have time. Check out my projects page for more information! I also enjoy playing the piano and getting out to the beach to enjoy the marine wildlife.
