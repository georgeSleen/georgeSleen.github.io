About me

I’m an Engineering Physics student at UBC, deeply fascinated by how electronics and physics shape the world around us. Whether I’m building a submarine with my classmates or designing PCBs for my custom computer, I’m always eager to experiment and learn more.

This year, I’m excited to lead SUBC, UBC’s submarine design team, as both Team Captain and Propulsion Systems Co-lead. We’re gearing up for the 18th International Submarine Races, and I’m determined to help our team innovate and take home the win. It’s an incredible experience to bring lots of brilliant students at UBC together and go from concept to a fully functional submarine!

That same drive to bring ideas to life fuels my personal projects as well—whether it’s building cloud chambers or coding Wi-Fi-enabled lights. I love solving problems and figuring out how to turn ambitious concepts into working realities.
